     About


    For this project, we were asked to create a digitalized illustration in isometric perspective.

     Date


    April 2024

     Software used

     Process & Challenges


    I first sketched in paper what I wanted to do and I decided to go for a minecraft pixely vibe landscape that has two levels. A overworld and an undeground world. To help me design in isometric, I made multiple grid guidelines on the canvas.

    The challending part of this project is making sure that I would be precise with the angles of my shape so that it remains isometric likes the blocked trees.


     Solution & Outcome


    To help me design in isometric, I made multiple grid guidelines on the canvas. I also used gradient colours to enhance the overall visual of the illustration.
