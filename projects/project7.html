    • Magazine Cover

     About


    This project required us to create a magazine cover with all of its written components - making it look like the real deal.

     Date


    October 2024

     Software used

     Process & Challenges


    I decided to do a magazine cover for Keshi - an artist who recently made an album after a two year haitus. I like his music and I knew he would fit well in a magazine cover. Before doing the actual magazine cover for Keshi, I had to identify the main components of a magazine cover so I took a sample of one and started anaylyzing it. I took an image from the artist's Instagram account and remove its background using Adobe PhotoShop and added a solid background colour that is somewhat neutral in order to not take away the spotlight from the main subject, using Adobe InDesign. I also added a drop shadow to the artist as an extra step and I think it fits well with the direction of the light from the picture itself.

    The challenging part of this project is probably thinking about the name of the magazine and the limited space I had to fit all the info that I wanted to put in the cover. I had to take into account the appropriate font size, colour and placement of each text components strategically rather than just randomly placing them in the magazine layout.


     Solution & Outcome


    I settled with the name "SOULWAVE" as Keshi's his music genre mostly revolves around having the soul vibe. In order to aligned my texts and graphics properly, I resorted to using InDesign for the grids and margins. I found a good way of balancing the information that I wanted to include in the magazine cover. I mainly chose red and white as the main colours and I chose the appropriate font size while respecting the grids and margins of the layout. I did want to include the artist's name somewhere and I decided to put it in the middle of the artist's name with a black background and this background is important because it highlights the name of the subject and making it stand out more. Additionally, his hands are also in the golden ratio, making it visually pleasing to the eyes, while also emphasizing his hands position - they put importance on his abilities to use musical instruments.
