    • Book Cover

     About


    For this project, we were asked to create book covers for three separate books that does not necessarily have any correlation towards one-another but to also pretend that they are written by the same author.

     Date


    April 2024

     Software used

     Process & Challenges


    I did my sketches in Illustrator and finalized the graphic elements there. Once I was satisfied with the outcome, I pasted the illustrations in InDesign while making sure I respected the dimensions of what a book looks like.

    The challenging aspect of this project is the fact that we had to make three different books that does not really have any correlation towards one another and make sure that there are returning elements for all three of the book covers.


     Solution & Outcome


    I decided to use important locations that the books are set in, and I used perspective to illustrate each of the locations. There is also always a moon and a bunch of clouds that are centered above that change colours based on the colour palette of the locations.
