    • Chocolate Box

     About


    This project required us to create either a box of chocolate or a bar of soap that is inspired by the Victorian Era. This included the fonts and the overall aesthetic of the box.

     Date


    May 2024

     Software used

     Process & Challenges


    I chose to do a chocolate box because I love chocolates and I ultimately decided to name it SOCOLA (phonetically sounds like Chocolat in French). I did most of the drawing in InDesign and made sure the designs were appropriate enough for the theme.

    The challenge was making it have a chocolate vibe while using graphic elements and a colour palette that were relevant to the theme of the project.


     Solution & Outcome


    I used a warm colour palette to give the vibe that I was looking for and used as less colours as possible and I used a font that has symbols to create some of the graphic elements of the box.
