    • E-Book

     About


    For this project, we were asked to create a story for children and illustrated it in a form of an E-Book. It needed at least 6 pages and a few animations within the graphic elements of the story.

     Date


    May 2024

     Software used

     Process & Challenges


    I brainstormed ideas on what kind of story can I come up with that is both kids friendly and is not too complicated to make. I came up wit ha story of a crow and a boy; it tells the story of how a kid and a crow repays each other’s kindness by helping one-another. I inspired myself by looking at pictures of a happy kid and the bird online and then do some drawings on Illustrators. Upon finishing all the graphic elements needed for the story, I gathered them all and put them in InDesign.

    The challenge comes with drawing the graphic elements as I purely used my mouse and keyboard to draw them. I would use the pen tool to draw specific lines, and it took some time to do all of them.


     Solution & Outcome


    To make sure I had enough time to accomplish this project, I started doing them all early and I let myself handle the extra details when I was done with the base design for example; gradients, animations, etc…
