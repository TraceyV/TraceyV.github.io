    • Trading Cards

     About


    We were tasked to create three trading cards that belong into a specific theme and make it look like an actual trading card with card info and reoccuring back cover

     Date


    January 2025

     Software used

     Process & Challenges


    I decided to go for natural disasters as the theme for my own trading cards, and I designed the cards by inspiring myself with their own power (or elements). An example of that would be the fact that a tornado would be a wind element because it is composed out of strong winds in real life. I added a fact for each natural disaster at the bottom of the card, and as for the back cover of the cards, it is made from all the symbols of all the elements that exist in my mind.

    The challenging part of this fun project was about coming up with drawings for each of the cards as I was just starting on the Micromedia program, and I have not really drawn digitally prior to that time.


     Solution & Outcome


    I looked at real life pictures of each of the natural disasters online and did a cartoonish version of each one. I used the pen tool to draw lines and used other tools in Illustrator to achieve the look that I was going for. I changed the typography from black to white for readability purposes and I was happy with the outcome in the end.
