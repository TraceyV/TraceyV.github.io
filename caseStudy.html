     Process & Challenges


    I wanted to do something that makes some kind of metaphor of giving a piece of yourself to other people or the other way around, and ultimately I decided to go for the latter as I am trying to reach potential donors into signing up to become one and it's their "piece of the puzzle" that completes someone else. I also chose to put the following tagline; save a life afterlife.

    The challenge about this project is about coming up with a way to integrate the chosen company's colour palette to the illustrations and making the overall design and messages easy to see, effective and attractive to the target audience.
